Nam Nguyen

Researcher - Industrial Engineer - Developer

I was an undergraduate research assistant focusing on fabrication and characterization of conductive polymer based gas sensor. I successfully fabricated fast response gas sensor by incorporating nanostructures into a conductive polymer matrix. To broaden my skill set, I designed a thermal solution for high-power LEDs bulb. My tasks involved heat transfer simulation, heat sink design, thermal interface material selection. Our team invented a method to reduce effectively LEDs temperature. As a Ph.D student, I am working on multi-functional composites. We are developing energy saving and scalable processing methods for multinational composite by integration of nanomaterials into polymer based matrix composite. We also study its structure-processing-property relationship at multiple length scales.
